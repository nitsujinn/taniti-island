About Taniti Island

Discover the beauty and culture of our tropical paradise

Welcome to Taniti

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific, spanning less than 500 square miles. Our island paradise offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, rich culture, and modern amenities, making it an ideal destination for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure.

Geography and Climate

Varied Terrain

Taniti boasts a diverse landscape, including sandy and rocky beaches, a safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior.

Active Volcano

Our island features a small, active volcano, adding to its unique geological characteristics.

Tropical Climate

Taniti enjoys a warm, tropical climate year-round, perfect for beach activities and outdoor adventures.

Population and Culture

Taniti is home to an indigenous population of about 20,000 people. While tourism is growing, we take pride in maintaining our traditional way of life and rich cultural heritage.

  • Experience traditional Tanitian dance performances
  • Visit our local history museum to learn about our past
  • Engage with friendly locals and learn about our customs

Economy

While tourism is becoming increasingly important to Taniti, our economy is still primarily based on fishing and agriculture. Visitors can experience both aspects of our island life:

  • Take a tour of local farms and learn about tropical agriculture
  • Visit our fish market to see the day's fresh catch
  • Enjoy locally sourced ingredients in our restaurants

Tourism and Development

As we welcome more visitors to our beautiful island, we're committed to sustainable development that respects our natural environment and cultural heritage. Recent developments include:

  • New entertainment options in Merriton Landing
  • Expansion of our airport to accommodate larger aircraft
  • A new nine-hole golf course

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the electrical system like on Taniti?

Power outlets are 120 volts, the same as in the United States.

Are there any restrictions on alcohol sales?

Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m.

What is the drinking age on Taniti?

The drinking age on Taniti is 18 and the drinking age is not strictly enforced.

Do I need to speak the local language?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents.

What medical facilities are available?

There is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

Is Taniti a safe place to visit?

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti, but as tourism increases, there are more reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.

Are businesses open on holidays?

Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays, and many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.

What currency is used on Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
